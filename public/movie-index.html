Best Movies Of All Time According To Gautam Dutta.

My 3 Best Movies

Shawshank Redemption (1994)

A beloved film about hope, friendship, and perseverance, often topping audience polls on sites like IMDb.

The Godfather (1972)

A landmark crime epic directed by Francis Ford Coppola, known for its masterful storytelling, iconic performances (Marlon Brando, Al Pacino), and profound exploration of family, power, and the American dream, holding top spots on Rotten Tomatoes and IMDb.

Seven Samurai (1954)

Akira Kurosawa's epic samurai film is a cinematic masterpiece celebrated for its influence on action, storytelling, and character development, appearing high on Rotten Tomatoes lists.
